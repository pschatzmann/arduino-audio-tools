Detailed Description

Analyzes audio stream quality by detecting clicks/pops, gaps/dropouts, and clipping/corruption.

Insert this stream into an audio pipeline to monitor signal quality in real time. Data passes through unmodified.

  • Clicks/Pops: detected when the sample-to-sample delta exceeds a configurable threshold (as a ratio of the maximum sample value).
  • Gaps/Dropouts: detected when consecutive near-zero samples exceed a configurable minimum length.
  • Clipping: detected when consecutive samples are at (or near) the maximum representable value.

Results are available via stats() or through a callback.
